Maurits Boettger

Works Vita

Maurits Boettger, born in 1990, Karlsruhe Germany
Studies at Academy of Media Arts Cologne, 2012-2017
DAAD-Scholarship for studies at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, 2014-2015

In his work Maurits Boettger questions the process of perception regarding time and space. Media is, what surrounds us in our everyday life: light, air and time. Aiming for more consciousness concerning our own perception, he uses techniques that slightly change our surroundings. Furthermore Boettger explores the differences between still- and moving images, continuity and interruption, contrasts and nuances. His work is presented in forms of videos, instruments, installations and interaktive applications.
