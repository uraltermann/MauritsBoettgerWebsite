Maurits Boettger

Works Vita

Maurits Boettger, born in 1990, Karlsruhe Germany
Studies at Academy of Media Arts Cologne, 2012-2017
DAAD-Scholarship for studies at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, 2014-2015

In his work Maurits Boettger questions processes in perception of time and space. Utilized Media is light, air, still- and moving images, installations, apps, and in a broader sense again: time. not seldom the media itself becomes the subject of investigation. In their observation spectators are reflected upon themselves.
