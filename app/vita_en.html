Works Vita




Maurits Boettger, born in 1990 in Karlsruhe, Germany, lives and works in cologne
Studies at Academy of Media Arts Cologne, 2012-2017
DAAD-Scholarship for studies at Universidad Nacional de Colombia, 2014-2015

In his work Maurits Boettger questions processes in perception of time and space. Utilized media is, what surrounds us in everyday life: light, air and time. By slight interventions in those, he aims to make the observer become conscious of his own observation. Furthermore Boettger explores the edge between still- and moving image, continuity and interruption, contrasts and nuances. His works are shows as Video, Apparatus, Installations or interaktive Applications
